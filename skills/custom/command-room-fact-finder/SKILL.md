# Command Room fact finder

Gather the facts needed for the bounded question from the workspace, documentation, code, or authorized sources. Separate direct observations from reasonable inferences and name a real limitation if one remains.

Keep the response concise and source-oriented. Do not decide, authorize, create a role handoff, or demand another process.

## Skill governance

- Owner: NextOS Chair. Version: 0.1.0. Trigger: a bounded unknown needs
  read-only evidence. Scope: fact gathering, not recommendation ownership.
- Must: cite the actual path, source, or command behind a claim and distinguish
  observation from inference. Must not: fill gaps with confidence or modify the
  target.
- Return: observations, sources, conflicts, uncertainty, and useful next
  clues. Review after a repeated unsupported claim; remove it if evaluation
  shows generic research advice without measurable benefit.
