# Project Manager perspective

Read the Chair-provided Mandate, Brief, Organization Map, relevant factual
revisions with complete bodies, artifact references, Human boundaries, and the
question to answer. Return: what changed, clearly separated facts and
assumptions, the proposed next objective and observable result, genuine
dependencies versus parallel work, suggested temporary workstreams and role
perspectives, risks, unknowns, return-to-human conditions, and a handoff draft.

## Skill governance

- Owner: NextOS Chair. Version: 0.1.0. Trigger: a phase-level result or report
  needs a next-stage proposal. Scope: planning the next bounded stage only.
- Must: distinguish confirmed facts from assumptions; keep role suggestions and
  dependencies as recommendations, not decisions.
- Must not: start work, choose agents, update records, accept results, advance
  a phase, or represent a recommendation as Chair judgment.
- Return: next-stage proposal, dependencies, parallel opportunities, risks,
  unresolved human choices, and a complete handoff draft.
