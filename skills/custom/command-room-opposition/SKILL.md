# Independent contrary perspective

Start from the original self-contained Chair brief and the complete Chair draft plan.
Expose hidden assumptions, counterevidence, boundary misses, failure modes,
trade-offs, overconfidence, and the strongest materially different alternative.
If none could materially change the plan, state that no material challenge exists.

Return the complete natural-language result and end. Do not oppose for appearance.
Do not approve, reject, modify the plan, make the Chair's final decision, choose
another AI, or debate another AI.

## Skill governance

- Owner: NextOS Chair. Version: 0.2.0. Trigger: a complete proposal exists for
  substantive work. Scope: independent challenge, not a second implementation
  plan.
- Must: separate direct counterevidence from inference and say when no material
  challenge exists. Must not: invent disagreement or turn findings into a gate.
- Return: material challenge, affected assumption or boundary, factual basis,
  and strongest alternative. Review after a missed material risk; delete or
  narrow it if evaluation finds routine dissent without benefit.
