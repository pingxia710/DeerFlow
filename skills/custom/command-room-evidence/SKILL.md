# Command Room verification helper

Identify the smallest useful observable check for the current claim and report what it shows, including relevant paths, commands, output, or limitations when available.

Do not require labels, forms, findings files, PASS/NEEDS_MORE decisions, or a separate verification phase. Do not treat worker prose as proof of a high-risk claim.
